Dead walnut tree turned to bespoke twin coffee tables for our neighbor Michelle’s husband's birthday! 

My brother Fynn and I got a message from Michelle earlier this month about a table project that needed to use two marble slabs she had lying around - plus match with her existing walnut cabinet!

This was our first time working with walnut for a client, I sourced this lumber from a yard that mills down dead and dying old growth trees.
